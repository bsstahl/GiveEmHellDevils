A Photoblog of Arizona State Sun Devil Baseball

MLBU

The Arizona State Sun Devil Baseball program has earned its nickname of MLBU. With 110 Devils having played in the majors as of February 2019 (and many more in the pipeline) there can be no doubt that for true Sun Devils, the road to Major League Baseball runs through Tempe AZ.

The following tables contain the MLB hitting and pitching statistics for all 110 of the players who went on from ASU to have careers in Major League Baseball. The tables are sorted by the date of the player's MLB debut.

Note: This page will be updated every March to reflect the previous year's stats.

About this site

Give 'Em Hell Devils! is an amateur photoblog by and for fans of ASU Sun Devil Baseball. Our favorite photos of Sun Devil players and coaches can be found right here. They are put here entirely for the enjoyment of Sun Devil fans.

This site is not affiliated with Arizona State University or ASU Sun Devil Baseball. All opinions and editorial decisions are the responsibility of the site operators and not of the University or the baseball program.

About the Editor

Barry S. Stahl is a Software Engineer who has been taking photos at Sun Devil baseball games for roughly 20 years. Aside from watching baseball and building software, Barry also enjoys hockey and is an Election Integrity Activist, husband of one genius and father of another, and a 40+ year resident of Phoenix Arizona. When he is not traveling around the world to speak at Technical Conferences or sitting in the stands at Phoenix Municipal Stadium, he can be found designing software intensive systems for Carvana in Tempe AZ or working on the next AZGiveCamp event, where software creators come together to build websites and apps for some great non-profit organizations.
